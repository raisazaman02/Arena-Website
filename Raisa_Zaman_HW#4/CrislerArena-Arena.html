Capacity and Concessions

Crisler Arena has a capacity of 12,707. It has concessions all around the concourse including drinks, pizza, popcorn, and pretzels. Local organizations can volunteer to operate a concession stand as a fundraiser.

  • Mezzanine
  • Premium
  • Skybox

History

Crisler Arena is "The House that Cazzie Built". Cazzie Russell helped Michigan basketball win three consecutive Big Ten Conference titles from 1964 to 1966. It was built in 1967 and named after Herbert "Fritz" Crisler, head football coach at Michigan from 1938 to 1947 and athletic director until his retirement in 1968. Your friendly course instructor played basketball there a few times.
